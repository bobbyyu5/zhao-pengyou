Privacy Policy — 找朋友 Zhao Pengyou (Find Friends)

Last updated: 2026. Replace the contact email below before publishing.

找朋友 Zhao Pengyou is a card game for friends and family. We collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • No accounts. To play online you enter a display name and a room code. These exist only in the game server's memory during your session and are not stored in a database or sold.
  • Video & audio (if you turn on the camera) is sent peer-to-peer between players. We do not record, store, or have access to it; our server only relays the connection setup.
  • No analytics or tracking on the web version.

Advertising (Android app only)

The Google Play version of the app shows ads via Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may collect and use device identifiers and related data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. In regions that require it (e.g. the EEA/UK), the app shows a consent form before any personalized ads. The web (browser) version shows no ads.

Children

The game is suitable for general audiences. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

You can play single-device vs. bots fully offline with no data leaving your device. For online play, simply don't enter a real name if you prefer. You can decline camera/microphone permission and still play.
